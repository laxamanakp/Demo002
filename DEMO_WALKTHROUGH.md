## **PART 3: Clinical Workflow (2 minutes)**

### Navigate to "Clinical Visits"
- Click "Record New Visit"
- **Show**: Comprehensive visit form
- **Quick fill**:
  - Select patient
  - Enter vitals: BP 120/80, HR 72, Weight 65, Height 170
  - **Point out**: BMI auto-calculates
  - Select WHO Stage
  - Add clinical notes
- **Submit**: "Visit recorded successfully"

> "Physicians can document complete clinical encounters with vital signs, WHO staging, and treatment plans."

### Navigate to "Prescriptions"
- Click "Create Prescription"
- **Quick fill**:
  - Select patient
  - Add drug: TLD, 1 tablet, Once daily, 30 days
  - Click "+ Add Another Drug"
  - **Point out**: Multiple medications supported
  - Set next refill date
- **Submit**: "Prescription created"

> "Digital prescriptions are printable, track refills, and automatically create medication reminders."

### Navigate to "ART Regimens"
- Click "Start New Regimen"
- **Quick fill**:
  - Select patient
  - Add drug from dropdown (from inventory)
  - Set pills per day
  - Enter initial pills dispensed
- **Submit**: "Regimen started"
- **Show**: Active status, pills remaining, days on ART

> "Complete ART monitoring - tracking every pill dispensed, pills remaining, and adherence."
